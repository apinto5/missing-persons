Missing Persons Report: Sydney Pinto

About the Case

On the night of 06/26/2022, Sydney Lee Pinto disappeared from her family's home at Branch Brook Campground in Campton; a small town in Grafton County, New Hampshire. According to her mother, Heather Pinto, she'd spent the day on a "girl's trip" with Heather and her best friend, Maura Harty. They returned home around 6:00 P.M. that evening, at which point the various members of the family set to their nightly routine; Maura went to spend time with Sydney's brother, Alex Pinto, while Heather and Pedro, Sydney's father, took to watching television. Sydney's absence wouldn't be noticed until the morning, but it's suspected she went missing around 8:00 P.M. while taking her dog for a walk on the family's property. Heather asserts that Sydney would usually walk the dog on the edge of the family's property, near what's dubbed "The Birches".
